Welcome to Muiz Hot Chicken HQ

Established on 1 May 2026, Muiz Hot Chicken was born from a simple vision: to create the crispiest, most flavorful halal fried chicken in Malaysia. Our secret? A 12-hour marinade using 12 local herbs and spices, plus a proprietary double-coating technique that locks in crunch.

Today, we're proud to be Malaysia's fastest-growing halal crispy chicken franchise, with 50+ outlets nationwide and a passionate community of franchise partners. Our corporate headquarters oversees quality, innovation, and the Muiz standard that customers love.
