Muiz Hot Chicken Franchise – Malaysia's Fastest-Growing Halal Crispy Chicken Brand

Over 232 outlets nationwide. Discover the franchise opportunity that has taken Malaysia by storm.

Muiz Hot Chicken is Malaysia's fastest-growing halal crispy chicken franchise, with over 232 outlets nationwide. From humble beginnings as a roadside stall in Parit Raja, Johor, in 2013, Muiz Hot Chicken has grown into a household name – loved by Malaysians for its 12-hour marinated, double-coated crispy chicken.
