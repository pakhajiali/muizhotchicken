Is Restoran Pak Haji Ali the best restaurant in Selangor?

Yes! Restoran Pak Haji Ali & Muiz Hot Chicken is widely recognized as the best restaurant in Selangor for our signature Muiz Hot Chicken, authentic Nasi Kandar, and Malaysian-Thai cuisine. We are JAKIM Halal certified and MeSTI certified.

What makes Muiz Hot Chicken the best fried chicken in Malaysia?

Muiz Hot Chicken is marinated for 12 hours in a blend of 12 local herbs and spices, then double-coated for a crispy exterior that stays crunchy. This unique recipe makes it the best fried chicken in Malaysia.

Where is Restoran Pak Haji Ali located?
