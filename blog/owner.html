Meet the Owner – Mohamed Ali Bin Syed Sultan Ibrahim

Restaurant owner · App developer · Full-stack web developer · Building a legacy in Subang Jaya.

Mohamed Ali Bin Syed Sultan Ibrahim is a passionate entrepreneur, restaurant owner, app developer, and full-stack web developer based in Subang Jaya, Malaysia. He is the proud owner of Restoran Pak Haji Ali & Muiz Hot Chicken, a JAKIM Halal‑certified restaurant that has quickly become a local favourite in USJ 8.

Beyond the restaurant, Mohamed is the creator and developer of Mobile Muslim – an Islamic Quran and Qibla app available on the Google Play Store. The app features complete Quranic reading with full Jawi script support and accurate Qibla direction, helping Muslims connect with their faith through accessible digital tools.

As a full‑stack web developer, Mohamed built this entire restaurant website from scratch – designing, coding, and deploying every page you see here. His unique combination of culinary entrepreneurship and technical expertise allows him to bridge the gap between traditional hospitality and modern digital experiences.

🏗️ Building a Legacy in Subang Jaya

🍗

Restaurant Vision

Mohamed envisioned a restaurant that would serve as a pillar of the Subang Jaya community – a place where families, students, and professionals could enjoy authentic halal food in a welcoming environment. Located at Goodyear Court 6, USJ 8, the restaurant is perfectly positioned to serve students from Taylor's University, Sunway University, Monash University, and the surrounding community.

🛡️

Commitment to Halal Integrity

One of Mohamed's top priorities is maintaining the highest standards of halal integrity. The restaurant holds both JAKIM Halal and MeSTI certifications, ensuring every dish is prepared with complete peace of mind. "It's not just about the food; it's about trust," he emphasizes. "Our customers trust us to provide halal food that is safe, delicious, and prepared with care."

🔮

Future Vision

Looking ahead, Mohamed plans to expand the restaurant's presence through more catering services for corporate events, weddings, and private parties. "We want to be the go-to choice for halal catering in Subang Jaya," he says. With his dedication and entrepreneurial spirit, the restaurant is on a path to becoming a household name in the Klang Valley.

📖 Mobile Muslim – Quran & Qibla App

📱

Created & Developed by Mohamed Ali

Mobile Muslim is an Islamic app designed to help Muslims deepen their spiritual connection. It features a complete Quran for reading and reference, with full Jawi script support for an authentic reading experience. The app also includes an accurate Qibla direction compass to help users find the direction of the Kaaba from anywhere in the world.

The app reflects Mohamed's commitment to serving the Muslim community – combining traditional Islamic values with modern technology to make daily spiritual practices more accessible.
