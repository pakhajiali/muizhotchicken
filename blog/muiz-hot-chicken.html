🇲🇾 The Story Behind Muiz Hot Chicken

Muhamad Muizzuddin Remle
Founder · Started at age 20

Muiz Hot Chicken was founded by Muhamad Muizzuddin Remle in Parit Raja, Johor, on 18 February 2013 – just three days after his 20th birthday. What started as a humble roadside stall has grown into Malaysia's fastest‑growing halal crispy chicken franchise, with over 232 outlets nationwide.

The recipe for the crispy, flavourful chicken was handed down from his aunt, and Muizzuddin perfected it over the years. Today, Muiz Hot Chicken is a symbol of local entrepreneurship, quality, and halal integrity.
